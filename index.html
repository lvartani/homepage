ABOUT

According to a study by the Republic of Armenia's RA National Academy of Sciences’ Center for Ecological-Noosphere Studies, mining in Armenia has been ruinous for Armenia in terms of public health and the environment.Mining operators have failed to neutralize dangerous contaminants which have been absorbed by the ground. The pollutants then pass from the ground to agricultural produce and then to humans. 57% of Yerevan's population, the capital city and most populous region, may be living in contaminated conditions due to ground pollution. Additionally, the country’s rural fields are being irrigated with water flowing from contaminated sources due to mining operations. Near copper processing plants, emission of sulfar dioxide, exceeds 15-20 times the maximum permissible concentrations in the ambient air, a serious hazard to human health. Also, according to the study by the RA National Academy of Sciences, all types of farm produce from the towns of Kapan, Kajaran, Alaverdi, and Akhtala (all of which have significant mining operations) are laden with heavy metals, including mercury arsenic and cadmium. As most of the mining done in Armenia is illegal, there is the call to geographically locate these exploited mines for residents to discover nearby locations and ascertain their risks from these mines.

This is a selection of print Geographical Information Systems (GIS) work samples. The project illustrates a comparative analysis of park space in Los Angeles in 2012 to the projected plans of LA green space designed by the Olmsted Brothers in 1930.

I am fascinated by cartographic representations of data and digital story-telling. I work to articulate complex subject matter through visualization methods –using statistical programming, data analysis, GIS mapping, and web design to communicate important data patterns through graphical representations. Particularly I am motivated by research relating to urban discussions for equitable community development, social justice, and sustainability.

When I am not making maps, I like to surf, hike, and watch the Oprah Winfrey Show. Also, I read mystery novels and practice my backstroke, reliving high school swim relay races, whenever I get the chance.
