Chile Student Population

About: With news of mass protests in Chile by students for education reform, I wanted to see where university students were coming from. Were they coming from mostly urban regions or rural areas? This diagram shows that most students are from the Santiago, Chile and the ones that are migrating, are relocating overwhelmingly to the metropolitan center of Chile.

Skills: D3 JavaScipt Library, jQuery, Bootstrap

Data: Data from Consejo de Rectores/ Anuario Estadistico 2012

Armenian Mine Data and Environmental Projects

About: According to a study by the Republic of Armenia's RA National Academy of Sciencesâ€™ Center for Ecological-Noosphere Studies, mining in Armenia has been ruinous for Armenia. Mining operators have failed to neutralize dangerous contaminants which have been absorbed by the ground. The pollutants then pass from the ground to agricultural produce and then to humans. A majority of the population may be living in contaminated conditions due to pollutants from the ground. As most of the mining done in Armenia is illegal, there is the call to geographically locate these exploited mines as a means for residents to discover nearby locations and become more aware of potential health risks.

Skills: D3 JavaScipt Library, jQuery (Left) Bootstrap, Excel, Google Fusion Tables, Fusion Tables Layer Wizard (Right)

Data: Data from Acopian Center for the Environment and Armenian Development Agency Research, 2011

Informal Settlement Mapper

About: The United Nations Development Programme defines informal settlements as residential areas where housing units have been constructed on land to which the occupants have no legal claim. Therefore posing problems of sanitation, health, overcrowding, and violence to the residing population. Through Google satellite view and UNDP data, this project involves researching and geolocating these informal settlements or slums throughout the world. Bounds or extents of the settlements are visible through exploration of the satelite view in the application.

Skills: Fusion Tables, Fusion Layer Wizard, Google Maps, Bootstrap

Data: United Nations Development Programme 2010 and CIA World Factbook API
